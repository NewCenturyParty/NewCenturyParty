> <p align="center"><strong>A political project dedicated to placing Human Dignity at the core of Canadian governance.</strong></p>

### Our Purpose

The New Century Party (NCP) is a conceptual framework and organizing hub for a new kind of Canadian politics. We believe the profound challenges of the 21st century require a bold, integrated, and fundamentally humane vision for the future, with Human Dignity at the heart of all we do.

This is the starting point of a movementâ€”one that seeks to build policy not from the top down, but from the foundational principle that a just society is one that upholds the dignity of every single person.

### Our Flagship Project: The 21st Century Act

The core of our work is the development and advocacy of **The 21st Century Act**. This is not just an Act; it is a comprehensive, open-source legislative blueprint for national renewal. It proposes a cohesive framework for tackling our economic, ecological, and democratic crises simultaneously.

We believe the solutions for our future must be built together, in the open.

### How to Get Involved

The New Century Party grows as the 21st Century Act becomes stronger, more resilient, and more human.
